Medical Topic: Diabetes
Overview: Diabetes is a condition in which blood sugar levels remain high due to problems with insulin production or insulin response.
Common Symptoms: Frequent urination, increased thirst, fatigue, blurred vision, weight changes, slow healing wounds.
What To Do: Monitor blood sugar, take medicines on time, follow diabetic diet, exercise regularly, attend medical follow-up.
What Not To Do: Do not skip medicines, do not consume excessive sugar, do not ignore foot care, do not miss regular checkups.
Precautions: Maintain healthy diet, exercise, monitor glucose, manage stress, inspect feet regularly, stay hydrated.
Doctor Focus: Assess glucose control, medication adherence, dietary habits, early complications, and long-term risk management.
Quick Summary: Diabetes needs regular monitoring, medicine adherence, dietary discipline, and early detection of complications.
